Second Eye is a free AI photography critique tool. Upload any photo — portrait, street, landscape, wildlife, architecture, macro or product — and get an overall score out of 10 plus six dimension scores: composition & framing, light & exposure, sharpness & technique, colour & processing, storytelling & emotion, and originality. Every critique ends with one concrete change to try on your next shot.

How does Second Eye score my photo?

It evaluates your photo across six dimensions — composition, light, sharpness, colour, storytelling and originality — and returns an overall score out of 10 with specific, honest feedback. The scoring criteria adapt to the genre you select.

Is Second Eye free?

Yes. You can upload a photo and get a full AI critique and score for free.

What feedback do I get with my score?

An overall score and per-dimension scores, what works in the image, what to improve, and one concrete change to try next — the candid feedback friends and likes never give you.
